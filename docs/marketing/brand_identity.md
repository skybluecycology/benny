# Benny Brand Identity & Visual Guidelines

## The Visual North Star
**"Complex Systems, Simplified Visuals."**

### Logo & Iconography
![Benny Logo](/C:/Users/nsdha/OneDrive/code/benny/docs/marketing/assets/logo.png)

The Benny logo represents the **Neural Nexus**—where individual "nodes" (agents) connect into a stable, structural whole. It should evoke feelings of stability, connectivity, and precision.

### 🐝 The "Digital Swarm" Aesthetic
![Digital Swarm Visual](/C:/Users/nsdha/OneDrive/code/benny/docs/marketing/assets/swarm_visual.png)

The **Digital Swarm** is our core visual metaphor for high-concurrency orchestration. 
- **The Storm:** Represents the raw, chaotic energy of autonomous agents (red, glitchy, unstructured).
- **The Lattice:** Represents Benny's deterministic control (cyan, geometric, glowing honeycomb).
- **Style:** Cyberpunk-adjacent, glassmorphism, high-contrast, and pulsing with data.

### Color Palette

| Color | Hex | Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Swarm Indigo** | `#0F172A` | Primary background, deep shadows. |
| **Nexus Cyan** | `#06B6D4` | Primary brand color, active states, connectors. |
| **Governance Gold** | `#F59E0B` | Critical checkpoints, human-in-the-loop nodes. |
| **Logic White** | `#F8FAFC` | Clean typography, high-contrast text. |

### Typography
- **Headings:** *Outfit* or *Inter* (Semi-bold). Modern, sans-serif, geometric.
- **Body:** *Roboto* or *Inter* (Regular). High legibility for technical docs.
- **Code:** *Fira Code* or *JetBrains Mono*. Precise, monospaced.

### Brand Voice
- **Authoritative:** We are the experts in agentic reliability.
- **Clear:** We don't use "AI Magic" buzzwords. We talk about "Deterministic DAGs" and "Lineage Tracking."
- **Enabling:** We don't replace developers; we give them the tools to manage swarms.
